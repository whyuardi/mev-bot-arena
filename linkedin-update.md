📌 **MEV Arena — Gamified MEV Bot Competition Platform**

Built during daily hackathon challenge.

A Web3 gamified simulation platform where users deploy MEV bots (Sandwich, Arbitrage, Liquidation, Flash Loan, Sniper) and compete in 30-second rounds to capture the highest block rewards.

**Features:**
• Interactive 3D block visualization (Three.js) — real-time mempool animation with orbiting camera
• Bot Creator — deploy custom bots with configurable strategy & capital allocation
• Real-time simulation engine — 30s competition rounds with live score updates
• Live leaderboard — sortable rankings, strategy filters, search, pagination with Recharts bar chart
• Smart contract (Solidity) — on-chain bot registration, round management, reward distribution (solidity ^0.8.20)
• Premium dark theme with crimson accent, glassmorphism, phosphor icons

**Tech Stack:** Next.js 16, TypeScript, Tailwind v4, Three.js, Solidity, Hardhat, Recharts, Phosphor Icons, Motion (Framer Motion v12)
